Premium

We pay extra for most high quality premium labels (i.e. Chanel, Ralph Lauren, Gucci, DKNY, Marc Jacobs, etc.) and vintage, boutique, or unique items.

Non-Premium

We resell most non-premium items between $6 - $12, so you will receive on average from $1.50 to $3 each in store credit or $.75 to $1.50 in cash for each piece.
